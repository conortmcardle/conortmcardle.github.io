About Me

I'm a Digital Communications Specialist at the MIT School of Engineering, where I develop and implement the school's digital communications strategy. I co-lead the redevelopment of engineering.mit.edu and serve as the school's leading expert in digital channels, strategies, and opportunities — reporting to the Executive Director of Communications.

Before this role, I spent over seven years as Video and Digital Content Specialist at MIT Project Manus, MIT's initiative to upgrade campus makerspaces and connect the MIT maker community. I created video, digital, and print content, and helped bring the Mobius app to life — a platform linking makers with resources across 40+ makerspaces and fabrication labs on campus.
